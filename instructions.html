Samurai Showdown
PLAY
instructions:
the Wind Hashashin is controlled via the WASD keys, left/right movement is a/d respectively. w is used to jump, and s for attack.
For the Fire Knight you use the arrow keys. left/right arrow for left and right movement, up arrow to jump, and down arrow to attack.
A time limit of 30 seconds is set to add pressure, and the last one alive is declared winner over all!
