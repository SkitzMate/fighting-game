Samurai Showdown
Instructions
"Samurai Showdown" is a captivating 2D retro fighting game set in an alternate world of feudal Japan, where two rival factions, the Fire Knights and the Wind Hashashin, clash for supremacy. Players step into the shoes of legendary warriors from each faction, engaging in intense battles which will affect the landscape of Japan for years to come. Who will reign victorious is anyones guess!
Characters:
The Wind Hashashin hails from the misty valleys of Shirakawa-go, Japan. Trained in stealth by the whispering winds, he moves with grace and precision, striking swiftly like a gust of wind. Clad in garments which blend seamlessly with the foggy terrain, he wields twin blades with deadly accuracy, his attacks as unpredictable as the shifting winds, making him a formidable opponent.
The Fire Knight emerges from the volcanic heart of Mount Fuji, Japan's iconic peak. Born amidst flames, he harnesses the fiery fury of the volcano in his combat style. Trained in the crucible of molten lava, he wields a blazing sword with unparalleled mastery. Clad in armor forged from the volcanic ores, he moves with the unstoppable force of an erupting volcano, striking fear into the hearts of his enemies. His attacks sear through opposition with relentless intensity.
